Overview

Purpose

The purpose of creating this website is to help people experience extraordinary rafting adventures!

Audience

Our target audience includes people aged 18 to 50 who are seeking thrilling outdoor experiences. Here are some scenarios:

  • Scenario: I'm planning a weekend with friends and want to try white water rafting for the first time. What options do I have?
    Answer: Our website provides information about rafting trips, including locations, trip durations, and what to expect during the experience.
  • Scenario: I would like to have this kind of experience; however, I would like to get more information about safety in general during the rafting experience. How does your company ensure a safe experience?
    Answer: Safety is our top priority. Our website shows our safety measures in detail, giving you peace of mind.
  • Scenario: I'm an experienced rafter looking for a new challenge. What advanced-level trips do you offer?
    Answer: On our website, you can explore our advanced trip options, including details on rapids classification, trip lengths, and the level of technical skill required.
